About Bead n Sprouts

Bead n Sprouts is a colourful handmade jewellery brand created to bring joy, creativity, and personality into everyday life. We make bracelets, necklaces, earrings, and nail sets that are bright, fun, and full of character.

Every piece is handcrafted with care using beads, charms, and colours that feel happy and expressive. We believe jewellery should be playful and unique — something that makes you smile when you wear it.

🌸 Our Mission

Our mission is to create affordable, handmade pieces that make people feel confident, creative, and special. We want every customer to feel the love and care put into each item.

🌼 Meet the Maker

Bead n Sprouts is run by a young creator with a passion for colours, crafting, and making people smile. Inspired by family, creativity, and small business life, every piece is made with heart.

🌟 Our Story

Bead n Sprouts began as a fun hobby and quickly grew into a small business. From simple bead bracelets to full collections of jewellery and nail sets, the brand continues to grow — one handmade piece at a time.
