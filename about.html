Frequently Asked Questions

Materials

What materials do you use?
We use high‑quality beads, charms, elastic, jewellery wire, and nail supplies.

Are your pieces safe for sensitive skin?
Most pieces are safe, but if you have allergies, let us know and we can adjust materials.

Do the colours fade?
Our beads keep their colour well when cared for properly.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Not yet — but we hope to in the future.

How much is shipping?
Shipping prices vary depending on the order size and location.

Care Instructions

How do I take care of my jewellery?
Keep pieces dry and store them safely when not in use.

Can I wear them in water?
We recommend removing jewellery before swimming or showering.

What if my bracelet stretches out?
Contact us — we can fix or restring it for you.

Yes, if it hasn’t been shipped yet.

We accept returns for damaged or incorrect items.
